Importance of Food Processing

Food processing is crucial for transforming raw agricultural products into safe, nutritious, and stable food items. It plays a vital role in:

  • Food Security: Ensures a stable supply of food throughout the year.
  • Reducing Food Waste: Extends the shelf life of perishable items.
  • Ensuring Safety: Eliminates harmful pathogens and contaminants.
  • Improving Nutrition: Enhances the nutritional value of food products.
  • Supporting Public Health: Contributes to overall health by providing safe food options.

Processing Techniques

Various food processing techniques include:

  • Canning
  • Freezing
  • Drying
  • Fermentation
  • Pasteurization

Each technique has its benefits and applications, helping to preserve food quality and safety.
