Key Processing Techniques

Canning, freezing, dehydration and other methods convert perishable crops into stable products for distribution and storage.

  • Canning — sealed and heat‑treated containers
  • Freezing — slows enzyme and microbial activity
  • Dehydration — moisture removal to inhibit growth

Preservation Methods

Refrigeration, fermentation, pickling and other preservation strategies keep food safe and palatable while maintaining quality.

  • Refrigeration — low temperature storage
  • Fermentation — beneficial microbes for flavor & safety
  • Pickling — acid or brine preservation
